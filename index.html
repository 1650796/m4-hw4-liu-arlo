Who is Charlie Zhou?

About | Discography | Awards

About

Charlie Zhou (周深), also known as Zhou Shen, is a Chinese singer-songwriter who is known best for his song, "Big Fish" from the animated film Big Fish and Begonia.

Charlie Zhou was born on September 29th, 1992 in Shaoyang, Hunan. After graduating high school, he went to Ukraine to study dentistry but later decided to transfer to Lviv National Music Academy to pursue his passion for singing instead. Since then, he has participated in several singing shows, such as The Voice of China, Super Vocal, The Masked Singer, among others. He has sung many OSTs for dramas, movies, and other films/shows. He has also performed live at many notable concerts.

Discography

As of 2022, Zhou has released 2 studio albums, 2 live albums, 1 EP, 30+ singles, 1 cover album, sung over 80 OSTS, and performed more than 23 other songs live.

Awards

Zhou has won an award every year since he debuted (not including the current year).

2021

  • Global Diplomats Chinese Cultural Night - 2021 Most Influential Young Singer
  • 2021 Television Series of China Quality Ceremony - Most Quality OST of the Year: Soften the Glare, Unify as Dust (和光同尘)
  • 2020 China Music Billboard Awards - Most Popular Male Singer, Most Popular OST: Only You (此生惟你)
  • Global Chinese Golden Chart Awards 2021 - Top 20 Songs of the Year: Who Am I to You (我是你的谁)
  • Tencent Music Entertainment Awards 2020 - Best Mainland Male Singer of the Year, Top 10 Songs of the Year: The Upside (触不可及)
  • QQ Music Year-End Charts 2020 - Top 10 Artists of the Year, Top 10 Singles of the Year: The Wind Rises (起风了)
  • Bilibili Music Awards 2020 - Most-Viewed Uploader of the Year, Musician of the Year
  • Uni Chart Annual Review 2020 - Top 10 Singers of the Year, Popular Songs Top 10 Singers of the Year
  • 2020 Tencent Entertainment Annual Grand Ceremony - Tencent Entertainment White Paper "The Most Popular Singer of 2020"
